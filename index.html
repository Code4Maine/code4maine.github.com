We Meet Often. Come Join Us.

View our Meetups site for our next location and time. We try to meet every few Weeks where we hack (develop) our projects, or brainstorm on current issues and activities around San Diego.

Want To Help Your Neighborhood, City or Region? Good, we could use your help.

We are a Code for America Brigade, which means we're volunteers that contribute our talents toward improving the way our local governments and community organizations use the web. Brigade is about action and you're here because you are someone who takes action. See what kind of things brigades do, or watch the inspirational TED talk given by Jennifer Pahlka, the founder of Code for America.

We Discuss Things Online. Join and Introduce Yourself.

Can't make it to a meetup? No problem. We collaborate on projects, ask for help, and post wins to our Code for Maine forum where you can get new posts in email and reply. We welcome anyone to sign-up... you might just see something you're interested in helping with.
